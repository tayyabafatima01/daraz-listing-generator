Tips & Guide

How to get the best results from your Daraz listings

📝
Be specific with features
Include dimensions, materials, colors, warranty, and compatibility. The more detail you provide, the better the AI can craft targeted keywords and descriptions.
🔍
SEO Title best practices
Daraz titles perform best at 80-120 characters. Include the brand, product type, key feature, and a size or color variant when applicable.
📦
Bullet points that convert
Each bullet should address a customer concern or highlight a unique benefit. Lead with the strongest selling point and end with warranty/after-sales info.
🏷️
Keyword strategy
Use a mix of broad (e.g. 'mobile phone') and specific keywords (e.g. 'Samsung 5G phone Pakistan'). Include both English and common Urdu transliterations when relevant.
📊
Test and iterate
Generate multiple versions by varying your feature descriptions. Compare titles and pick the one that best balances clarity, keyword density, and appeal.
